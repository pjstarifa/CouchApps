Introduction

Welcome to the SuperTool Admin Portal. Here you can manage all aspects of SuperTool within your scope of operations. Below are descriptions of the key concerns of our business along with links to the appropriate pages.

People

Here we can find anyone associated with TD Tom Davies and then link to their order history, contact details, contact history, and etc.

Products

Here we can find all of the products offered by TD Tom Davies. We can start an order process or look for products that are in production.

Orders

Here we can look at current orders and search for orders for opticians and customers.

Production

Here we can see all aspects of items in production. We can also link from the production item to the person who its being produced for or the product that is being produced.

Logistics

Here we manage the supply chain and link to warehouses, rep's bags, opticians, inventory, and goods in transit. We can search for items by Person or Product.

Credit Control

Here we look at Products that have been ordered and delivered but not yet paid for. We can link to the history of the person who made the order.

Summary

So how are we doing. Here we can look at reports on all sorts of stuff that will be of interest to us. Here we have access to graphs and reports showing out progress.

Feedback
